Experiences

Water Activities

Scuba diving, surfing, deep diving and calm‑water fishing across turquoise lagoons and coral reefs.

What It Feels Like

Lakshadweep’s lagoons are shallow and protected by reefs, which makes them perfect for first‑time swimmers as well as certified divers. From gentle snorkelling to deep‑water dives, every activity is centred around clear water and colourful marine life.

Instructors and boat crews are usually local experts who understand the sea, tides and safety requirements in detail.

Highlights

  • Introductory and deep scuba diving with certified instructors.
  • Surfing small, clean waves in selected island spots.
  • Lagoon snorkelling over coral gardens and reef fish.
  • Traditional boat‑based fishing trips at sunrise or sunset.

Good to Know

Best Season

Usually October – March, depending on local weather and sea conditions.

Safety

Always follow the instructions of certified guides and use the safety gear provided.

For Beginners

Calm lagoons are ideal for people who are new to the ocean or not strong swimmers.

Booking

Plan through your resort or authorised tour operator in advance.
