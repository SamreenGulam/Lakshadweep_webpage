Islands to Explore

Discover the finest tropical escapes of Lakshadweep

Agatti

Agatti Island with airstrip and a stunning lagoon, gateway to Bangaram.

Flights · Lagoon views · Island hopping

Minicoy

Southernmost island with lighthouse views and unique cultural flavour.

Lighthouse · Tuna fishing · Culture

Bangaram

Tourism atoll with turquoise lagoon, resort stays and night skies.

Resort · Stargazing · Lagoon

Kavaratti

Capital island with mosques, waterfront promenades and calm lagoons.

Promenade · Mosques · Calm lagoon

Amini

Historic atoll known for coir products, handicrafts and quiet lanes.

Culture · Crafts · Heritage

Kadmat

Long beaches with shallow waters on one side and deep sea on the other.

Snorkelling · Long walks · Resorts

Kalpeni

Shallow lagoon and islets ideal for lagoon sports and reef walks.

Kayaking · Shallow reefs

Chetlat

Small island with coconut groves and a close-knit community.

Quiet · Coconut landscape

Andrott

One of the largest islands with mosques, greenery and village life.

Local life · Mosques

Kiltan

Island with seafaring history on ancient trade routes.

Heritage · Calm ambience

Bitra

Tiny inhabited island offering wide ocean horizons and solitude.

Remote · Minimal footprint

Travel Info

Plan how you arrive and where you stay in Lakshadweep

Ways to Get Here

Flights

Limited flights operate to Agatti from Kochi. From Agatti, onward travel to other islands is by speedboat or ferry, as per permits and packages.

Passenger ships

Government‑approved passenger ships sail from Kochi on scheduled departures, offering basic cabins and decks to several islands.

Inter‑island vessels & boats

High‑speed crafts, ferries and local boats link Agatti, Kavaratti, Minicoy and other islands, depending on weather and approvals.

Stay

Island resorts

Beachfront resorts on islands like Bangaram, Kadmat and Kavaratti with all‑inclusive holiday packages and lagoon views.

Hotels & lodges

Simple, comfortable hotels and lodges on inhabited islands – ideal for budget travellers and longer stays.

Guesthouses & homestays

Family‑run stays that offer local food, culture and everyday life in Lakshadweep (only on select islands, with permits).
