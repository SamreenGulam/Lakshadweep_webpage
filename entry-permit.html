Entry Permit

Most visitors require an entry permit to visit Lakshadweep. Please review the latest process and official guidelines before planning your journey.

Permit Process – Overview

1

Confirm your category

Entry rules may differ for residents, Indian tourists, officials and other categories.

2

Choose islands & dates

Prepare a tentative travel plan. Permits are usually island‑specific and date‑specific.

3

Apply through authorised channel

Applications must be submitted through the designated tourism office or official portal.

4

Carry permit & ID

Keep digital and printed copies of your approved permit and a government‑issued ID.

Official Permit Portal

Entry permit information is hosted on the official Lakshadweep Government portal. Always verify details through authorised sources before applying.
