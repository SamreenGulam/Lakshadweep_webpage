Culture, Arts & Mosques

Discover the heritage, craftsmanship, architecture, and soulful traditions of Lakshadweep.

1

Traditional Arts & Crafts

Lakshadweep is known for its handwoven coconut leaf products, coir crafts, traditional jewellery, and ornate household artifacts crafted lovingly by island artisans.

2

Local Markets & Shopping

Explore lively markets offering handmade souvenirs, cultural ornaments, lacquer work, seashell jewelry and unique island delicacies.

3

Historic Mosques

Visit century‑old mosques known for their coral‑stone architecture, intricate wood carvings, and peaceful ambience — reflecting the deep cultural identity of the islands.

4

Performances & Island Traditions

Experience folk dances, rhythmic drumming, festive rituals and age‑old ceremonies that bring Lakshadweep’s stories to life.
