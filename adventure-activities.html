Adventure Activities

Parasailing, kayaking and other active ways to experience the lagoons and sea breeze.

For the Adventurous

Some islands offer additional adventure activities such as parasailing above the lagoon, kayaking through calm waters and other guided adrenaline experiences.

These activities are usually operated by licensed providers with safety checks, equipment and instructors on site.

Action from Above & On the Water

Feature a video of parasailing launches, kayaking or other high‑energy moments.

Highlights

  • Parasailing with wide aerial views of lagoons and reefs.
  • Kayaking in calm sections of the lagoon or along the shoreline.
  • Option to combine water sports in half‑day packages.
  • Guided sessions with safety gear and pre‑activity briefing.

Good to Know

Fitness

Basic fitness and comfort in water/height may be required for certain activities.

Weather

Parasailing and similar activities depend heavily on wind and sea conditions.

Age Limits

Operators may specify age and weight limits for safety reasons.

Advance Booking

Slots can be limited; pre‑book during peak season.
