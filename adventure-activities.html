Adventure Activities

Experience Lakshadweep from the sky, the shore, and the wild — thrilling adventures for every explorer.

1

Paragliding

Soar above turquoise lagoons and white-sand beaches as warm island winds lift you into Lakshadweep’s open sky. One of the most breathtaking aerial experiences in India.

2

Kayaking

Glide peacefully through calm lagoons, mangroves, and reef edges. Perfect for beginners and nature lovers seeking quiet exploration.

3

Lagoon & Island Trekking

Walk across shallow lagoon beds, explore untouched nature trails, and witness serene sunrise points surrounded by tropical wildlife.

4

Windsurfing

Harness the strong coastal winds and glide across blue lagoons. Ideal for thrill-seekers and intermediate adventurers craving speed.
