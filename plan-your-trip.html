Plan Your Trip

Whether you’re dreaming of turquoise lagoons, coral dives, cultural walks, or peaceful island stays, this guide helps you shape a trip that feels effortless and meaningful. Explore essential travel steps, stay options, and local experiences that bring Lakshadweep’s charm to life.

Trip planning essentials

1. Check eligibility & permits

Confirm who’s travelling, gather documents, and initiate the permit application in advance.

Linked with the Entry Permit section and official portal.

2. Align travel & stay

Match ship or flight bookings with resorts/guesthouses and inter‑island connections.

Coordinate with seasonal schedules and island availability.

3. Travel responsibly

Protect coral reefs, respect local customs and follow all safety guidelines.

Focus on low‑impact, eco‑aware travel across fragile atolls.

Island ambassadors & local guides

Why choose a local guide?

Local guides help you understand customs, choose respectful photo spots, and discover lesser-known experiences like traditional fishing or folk art.

  • Certified nature guides – reefs, birdlife, ecology.
  • Cultural ambassadors – stories, mosques, festivals.
  • Activity hosts – snorkelling, kayaking, village walks.
  • Local experts – crafts, folk art, traditions.
