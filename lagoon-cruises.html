Experiences

Lagoon Cruises

Slow travel across calm turquoise lagoons, sandbanks and reef edges.

On the Water

Lagoon cruises give you time to simply be on the water—watching colour changes, coral patches and distant islands as the boat moves gently across the lagoon.

Trips may include sandbank stops, swimming sessions, picnics or quiet sunset rides, depending on the island and operator.

Lagoon Journeys

Use this space for a video that captures boat rides and wide lagoon views.

Highlights

  • Boat rides over shallow turquoise lagoons and reef edges.
  • Stops at sandbanks or quiet beaches for photos and walks.
  • Sunrise or sunset lagoon cruises with changing sky colours.
  • Opportunities to spot fish, rays and sometimes dolphins.

Good to Know

Duration

Short one‑hour rides to half‑day trips, depending on itinerary.

What to Carry

Sun protection, light clothing, water, camera and a light cover‑up.

Safety

Life jackets are usually provided and should be worn when advised.

Bookings

Check availability with your resort or authorised operator on the island.
