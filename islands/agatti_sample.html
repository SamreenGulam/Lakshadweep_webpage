About Agatti

Agatti Island is the primary entry point to Lakshadweep, home to the only airport in the archipelago. Known for its shallow lagoons, coral reefs, and long sandy beaches, it blends local island life with pristine marine beauty.

The island is ideal for first‑time visitors, offering easy connectivity, snorkelling lagoons, and access to nearby islands such as Bangaram.
