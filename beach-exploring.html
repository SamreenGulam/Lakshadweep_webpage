Beach Exploring

Quiet walks, lighthouses and viewpoints where land, lagoon and ocean meet.

Slow Island Time

Beach exploring in Lakshadweep is less about crowds and more about long stretches of sand, coconut shadows and the sound of waves.

Lighthouses, viewpoints and small piers offer wide views of the sea, especially at sunrise and sunset.

Scenes from the Shore

Highlights

  • Walks along quiet beaches with lagoon views on one side.
  • Visiting lighthouses and viewpoints overlooking the open sea.
  • Sunrise and sunset photography spots.
  • Time to relax, read or simply watch the changing tide.
